About Me

Hello My name is Essien Emmanuel, I am Front-End Web Developer based in Nigeria, with a background in Computer Engineering from Madonna University. My journey into tech began with a fascination for how things work, which naturally led me to the world of web development. Over time, I have developed a passion for building responsive, user-friendly websites and web applications that combine clean design with strong functionality.
